**Leave Request Code in Python:**
```python
class LeaveRequest:
    def __init__(self, name, college_id, department, leave_type, leave_from, leave_to, number_of_days, reason_for_leave):
        self.name = name
        self.college_id = college_id
        self.department = department
        self.leave_type = leave_type
        self.leave_from = leave_from
        self.leave_to = leave_to
        self.number_of_days = number_of_days
        self.reason_for_leave = reason_for_leave

    def __str__(self):
        return f"Leave Request from {self.name} for {self.number_of_days} days from {self.leave_from} to {self.leave_to} due to {self.reason_for_leave}"

leave_request = LeaveRequest("Anshul Chauhan", "C001", "Computer Science", "Sick Leave", "2024-05-22", "2024-05-25", 4, "Health Condition")
print(leave_request)
```

**College Sick Leave Policy:**

1. A student may take a maximum of 5 sick leaves in an academic year.
2. The student must submit a leave application to the college authorities at least 2 days prior to the leave.
3. The student must provide a medical certificate from a registered doctor for a sick leave.
4. The student must complete all their assignments and tasks before taking a sick leave.
5. The student must make arrangements for coverage during their absence.
